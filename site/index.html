Welcome to MichelleP's Website

I’ve worked as an English as a Second Language teacher for the past 11 years. I love teaching, but due to the uncertainty of working as an independent contractor,
I decided it was time for a change and chose to pursue a long-time dream to return to school for computer programming this fall. In the early 1990’s I bought my first computer.
I taught myself everything I could about the software and hardware including upgrading my computer myself. I’ve been the family and friends help desk for years!
I learned enough about coding html to create a personal homepage. Many years later, I created a personal WordPress site which I maintained for a long time.

There are so many options to choose from in software development and IT, so I’m not yet certain which pathway I want to take. I’m sure I’ll have a better idea this time next year.
I’ve often considered computer game development because I love gaming, especially World of Warcraft, which I’ve been playing since 2007, but I’m also curious about cybersecurity,
AI and coding for robotics.

My Favorite Things

Music has always been a huge part of my life. Metal, rock and SKA are my preferred genres. I love Linkin Park (old and new), Iron Maiden, Dio, Bad Religion to name a few.
World of Warcraft is my long-time game of choice. No other game has captivated or kept me coming back like WoW has. Another of my favorite pastimes is listening to audiobooks!
I’m addicted to GraphicAudio. They produce dramatized versions of audiobooks that are played by a full cast. They’re fantastic! It’s a bit like the old radio shows in the pre-television era.
It’s like a movie in your mind and brings the books to life.
